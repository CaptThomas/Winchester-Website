Winchester Boat Club

The Winchester Boat Club is a great place to go sailing right here in Winchester! If you have a membership, you can take out a 420 and sail on the Upper Mystic Lake.

Community Boating, Inc.

This option is a bit farther off than WBC, but they have a lot of boats available. Mostly, people sail Mercury boats, but there are 420s available also.
